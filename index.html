OUR VISION

Small data plan should not cost big fortune.

To eliminate bandwidth scarcity and make rich interactive media accessible to everyone from everywhere. Most of the world budgets their Internet traffic because their usage is tied to their bill. By building new networks that support applications that utilize resources more efficiently while maintaining privacy, we can reduce network costs that can be passed to end users in the form of savings and a better user experience.

Content delivery networks help publishers reach users at scale by serving traffic from various ISP points of presence (PoP), also helping reduce peering costs for the network operator. However, current appliances which are typically deployed at ISPs require gigabit speed connectivity which prevents remote businesses and communities on low throughput connections from leveraging the savings of caching. With our regional delivery systems, content can be broadcast to end users allowing object storage of relevant resources and media access control.

Network Operators

Particularly at the regional level there is significant portion of traffic that is frequently accessed. By allowing publishers to utilize broadcast delivery and pre-caching to the end user, network operators can scale their operations and reduce peak traffic contention.

Application Developers

Write and deploy applications to the edge as you would for a datacenter with unprivileged signed container images that can be efficiently upgraded. The virtualized broadcast network overlay allows for traffic to be tagged and encrypted ensuring content remains relevant and private.

Residential Customers

Whether you are in a remote community, a vessel or an oil rig should not limit you from accessing the information you want. Publishers can deliver you subscription content in the nighttime, and tune into shared streams to reduce buffering times and spare your wallet.
