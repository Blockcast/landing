Our Vision

Different way to access internet.

Blockcast is a low cost open source asymmetric satellite broadband solution that can upgrade the link speed of existing connection by leveraging satellite television broadcasts. We tackle bandwidth scarcity using satellite broadcast streams to accelerate downloads, and blockchain to share the use of high throughput broadcast links.

High latency networks are a real barrier of entry to developing world. Blockcast provides resilient, fast access to internet, independent of connectivity

Something Different, Something New.

Traffic flow from client to Blockcast box to VPN proxy and response via satellite.

Increditble coverage and accessibility

A single satellite carrier covers up to a third of the globe and can broadcast at rates exceeding 160Mbps. With about 90% of user traffic being downloads, this solution can convert a low throughput terrestrial connection into an uplink to introduce broadband service in areas with restricted or limited connectivity.

Open sourced Access Point

The access point is an open source, managed software switch which can receive configurations via satellite to establish a secure connection to a cloud based OpenVPN proxy. Clients can begin issuing internet requests through the proxy after authentication. Requests are handled by the proxy and responses are encrypted over the VPN. Because the downlink is only one TTL from the access points, this network topology allows for leveraging broadcast and multicast messages to cost effectively deliver or zero rate cacheable content such as the bitcoin blockchain or popular videos.
