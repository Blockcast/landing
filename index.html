ABOUT US

Serving private, relevant bandwidth closer

Blockcast is an edge delivery service that improves network application performance and increases usability even when running over constrained networks such as DSL/Satellite/GPRS. Serving TCP/IP unicast real-time media during peak hours is straining to last mile internet service providers (ISPs) and results in poor browsing experience that decreases user engagement. Blockcast provides application developers and publishers with a secure environment to serve content locally at the consumer premises.

OUR VISION

Small data plan should not cost big fortune.

Our mission is to eliminate bandwidth scarcity and make interactive media accessible to everyone. Most of the world budget their Internet traffic because their usage is tied to their bill. By serving requests from the local network, end users can save on network transit costs, enjoy a richer browsing experience that doesn't compromise privacy, and reduce peak hour requirements for ISPs.
